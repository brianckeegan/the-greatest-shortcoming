Brian C. Keegan, Ph.D. is a computational social scientist and an associate
professor of information science at the University of Colorado Boulder.
He received undergraduate degrees from MIT, M.A. and Ph.D. degrees from
Northwestern University's School of Communication, and completed post-doctoral
training at Northeastern University and the Harvard Business School. He
currently resides in Boulder, Colorado with his wife and two sons.
